Welcome to Divisional School

At Divisional School, we believe that education is the most powerful tool to change the world. Serving students from Class 1 through Class 8, we provide a supportive and challenging environment that encourages intellectual growth and personal development.

Our curriculum is designed to spark curiosity and foster a love for learning, ensuring our graduates are well-prepared for the challenges of higher education and life beyond the classroom.

  • ✓ Holistic Primary Education (Class 1-5)
  • ✓ Advanced Middle School Curriculum (Class 6-8)
  • ✓ Focus on STEAM and Character Building

Our Academic Programs

We offer a structured and engaging learning path tailored to different developmental stages.

Primary School (Class 1-3)

Focusing on foundational literacy, numeracy, and social skills. We make learning fun through activity-based education and creative exploration.

Upper Primary (Class 4-5)

Transitioning to more formal academic disciplines while maintaining a strong emphasis on critical thinking and project-based learning.

Middle School (Class 6-8)

A rigorous curriculum preparing students for secondary education. Subjects include Advanced Mathematics, Sciences, Literature, and Social Studies.
